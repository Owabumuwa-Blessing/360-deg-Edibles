Our Story

360° Edibles is a fast-growing food brand dedicated to producing and delivering tasty, crunchy, and satisfying local snacks, drinks, and meals. What started as a passion for creating enjoyable, quality treats has evolved into a trusted brand known for consistency, rich flavor, and high hygiene standards.

We specialize in locally loved products such as kulikuli, zobo, tigernut drink, and yoghurt. Each crafted with care to preserve traditional taste while meeting modern packaging and quality expectations. Our goal is simple: to satisfy cravings anytime, anywhere.

MISSION STATEMENT

To provide high-quality, hygienically prepared, and delicious local snacks, drinks, and meals that satisfy cravings while promoting the richness of indigenous flavors

VISION STATEMENT

To become a leading household name in Nigeria and beyond for premium local snacks, drinks, and meals renowned for quality, innovation, and exceptional customer satisfaction.
